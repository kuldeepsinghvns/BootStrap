Interesting Facts in Mahakumbh

World's Largest Gathering: The Mahakumbh Mela is the largest religious and cultural gathering in the world, attracting millions of devotees.
UNESCO Recognition: In 2017, the Kumbh Mela was recognized as an "Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity" by UNESCO.
Astronomical Connection: The dates of the Mahakumbh are determined by the unique alignment of the Sun, Moon, and Jupiter.
Sangam's Mystical Importance: The confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati is considered the ultimate place for attaining salvation.
A Floating City: The Mela transforms into a temporary city with specially built tents, bridges, and other facilities to accommodate millions.
