Philosophy

Sant Atulanand Convent School

We believe children are as innocent and uninvolved as soil unless their thoughts are coloured with preconceived notions and prejudices prevalent in the surroundings. They can be given any constructive shape provided they are in skillful, learned hands. Following this concept, our institution with a secular backdrop and mass appeal works on the 6 principles of IDEALS (Intelligence, Diligence, Endurance, Adventure, leadership and Service) to unearth the hidden talents of every single child, stamps the young minds with SEAL (Social and Emotional Aspects of Learning) so that they are oriented towards SACS (Social Amelioration for a Cohesive Society) as responsible citizens of tomorrow with core global humanistic values and the intellectual heartbeat.
